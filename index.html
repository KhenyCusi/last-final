Toyota Supra

The fourth-generation Toyota Supra offered two engines: a naturally aspirated 3.0-liter inline-six and a more powerful twin-turbocharged variant. It was well-known for its high-performance capabilities, particularly in the Turbo edition, which produced plenty of horsepower and torque. A 6-speed manual transmission was standard, with an optional 4-speed automatic transmission. The MKIV Supra's trademark design maintained with its sleek and aerodynamic silhouette. The 2001 Supra is culturally significant, having achieved legendary status among automobile aficionados and garnering fame due to appearances in films like as "The Fast and the Furious."

Nissan Gtr R34

The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is a high-performance sports car produced from 1999 to 2002. It is part of the iconic Skyline GT-R series known for its dominance in motorsports. The R34 is powered by a 2.6-liter twin-turbo inline-six engine, producing impressive horsepower and torque. It features the ATTESA E-TS Pro all-wheel-drive system and the Super-HICAS four-wheel steering system for exceptional handling. With its distinctive design and motorsport success, the R34 has become a cult classic among automotive enthusiasts.

Mitsubishi Evolution 9

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution IX, often referred to as Evo 9, is a high-performance sports sedan produced from 2005 to 2007. It is the ninth and final iteration of the Evolution series. The Evo 9 is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine, delivering strong horsepower and torque. Equipped with Mitsubishi's Super All Wheel Control (S-AWC) system, it offers advanced traction and handling capabilities. The Evo 9 is known for its rally-inspired design, aggressive styling, and continued success in motorsports.
